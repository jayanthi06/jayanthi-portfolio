About Me

  • Automation & DevOps: Built CI/CD pipelines (Jenkins, Docker) and automated workflows using Python and Power Automate.

What sets me apart is my ability to bridge the gap between data and decision-making. Whether it's developing an automation framework for Infotainment ECU, predicting used car prices with machine learning models, or creating interactive Power BI dashboards for healthcare analytics, I thrive on delivering solutions that are both innovative and impactful.

I am passionate about leveraging data to drive positive change. From advanced analytics to designing an Autism Support App that enhances learning experiences for children, I am committed to using technology to make a difference.

When I'm not coding or analyzing data, you'll find me exploring the latest trends in AI, contributing to open-source projects. I believe in continuous learning and collaboration, and I'm always excited to take on new challenges that push the boundaries of what's possible with data.
